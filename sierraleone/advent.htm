February Concerts by the Freetong Players There were six public concerts held during the visit of the Freetong Players to Ottawa. Each event was hosted by a different church in a different part of the city. All funds raised are to be used for projects in Sierra Leone and to support the tour of the Players

"I'm A Friend of Sierra Leone" buttons are now available for sale at a suggested price of $1 each. There will be other designs available later as well as stickers.

top
March "Plant a Garden Of Hope" Campaign We invite groups to sell Spring-planted bulbs from Vesey Seeds in P.E.I. as a fund raiser for Sierra Leone. There are information packages available by contacting Dave Hall at dfhall@magma.ca. Each group makes their own arrangement with Vesey's and can keep 50% of all sales for their particular project.

If your group involves High School students, Canadian Friends of Sierra Leone will certify volunteer hours towards the forty hours needed to graduate. These hours will be based on a ratio of 1 hour volunteer time for every two orders obtained, paid for and delivered.

top
April Sierra Leone National Day Reception On Thursday April 24th, at Ottawa City Hall, Marion Dewar and Flora MacDonald will host a reception in honour of Sierra Leone's National Day. We hope to have the High Commissioner of Sierra Leone to Canada and the U.S. with us as a special guest.

There will be entertainment from the Northwestern Youth Choir and other groups plus a buffet of finger foods including samples of Sierra Leone cooking.

You can get your invitation by writing to Peacefund Canada and including your donation of $25 per person.
