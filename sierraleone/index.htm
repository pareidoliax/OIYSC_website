Canadian Friends of Sierra Leone 
A network to support the people of Sierra Leone as they struggle to rebuild their country after almost ten years of civil war. 


 

We warmly invite you to join us. There are several groups and individuals, based in Ottawa, who presently form the Canadian Friends of Sierra Leone network 

You can join in our activities as we celebrate, as we learn more about peace and conflict issues in Africa and as we raise funds for specific projects. You can find news of our activities and projects in Sierra Leone on this web site. 

Keep in touch and get involved!

Our Objectives
  • To raise awareness about the current situation in Sierra Leone 
  • To raise funds for the rebuilding of the country through the efforts of our partners in Sierra Leone. These include the S.L. YMCA and Freetown Y Service Club, the Network Movement for Justice and Development (NMJD) and the African Voluntery Services (AVS)
  • To link up with and support other groups across Canada who share the same concerns.
