OUR FIRST   2 - YEAR PROJECT IN SIERRA LEONE. (This could also go on another page)
                                Building a Culture of Peace in Sierra Leone:

Three Sierra Leonean organizations have joined together to implement a co-ordinated and holistic programme to provide the base that communities need to build a peaceful society. A group of Canadian organizations as the Canadian Friends of Sierra Leone, has pledged to support them in this effort. The Sierra Leonean organizations have identified three areas of priority for these first three years: Emergency Relief and Reconstruction, Peace and Development Education, and Micro projects.

Emergency Relief and Reconstruction: The programme will build on the community animators and their networks that are already in place to use constructive elements of Sierra Leonean culture and local materials and skills. Relief and reconstruction activities can build self-esteem and community coherence as well as dealing with immediate physical needs such as food and shelter. Reconstruction of schools and health centres is also planned.

Peace and Development Education: This component brings members of all sectors of a community together, even people, especially youth, who were once on opposite sides of a violent confrontation. Together they develop an understanding of the needs of every part of the community and plan methods of emergency relief, reconstruction and disaster preparedness that build reconciliation and a climate of co-operation. Skills of non-violent conflict resolution, understanding of civil rights obligations and responsibilities, and fundamentals of good business practice are part of the experience over the three year period.

Micro Projects: This strategy makes start-up capital and small project management training available to grassroots groups and organizations with the intention of improving food security, and providing employment through small enterprises.
