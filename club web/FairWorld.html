COFFEE BEANS

From Beanfair Coffee.

Our beans are from a Mexican co-op, and are certified fair trade and organic. The coffee is shade grown between other plants which means important habitat is provided for birds and other wildlife. The decaf is from Peru and is also certified organic/fair trade. It is naturally decaffeinated using high pressure CO2 – considered the best method for taste and the environment. We use bags with one-way valves that allow the beans to off-gas with no oxygen getting in, for ultimate freshness that lasts!

Product Descriptions.

Note: All balls are hand sewn with 5 ply twisted polyester thread. They are also guaranteed against manufacturing defects in the stitching. Most balls contain butyl bladders which should stay inflated for 30 days.

Soccer balls (Outdoor) Four levels of quality.

Team/School quality. Sizes 3 and 4 usually for children up to 12 years in recreational leagues with size 5 for teens. Balls have a 6 month guarantee and contain a butyl bladder.

Match/Training quality. For teen games and adult practice, also with a 6 month guarantee and using a butyl bladder and valve. Outer material is local PU Printed Shine with 4 polyester linings and a weight of 420-440g.

Premier match quality: For adult practice and games, with a 2 year guarantee and using a butyl bladder. Outer material is Japanese Cordley Shine with 4 polyester linings (4), circumference 69-70 cm and weight 430-445 g.

Professional quality: Our top quality with a 2 year guarantee using a butyl bladder. Outer material is Austrian PU Shine with air mattress and 3 polyester linings,circumference 69-70 cm and weight 430-445g.

Soccer balls (Indoor)
Indoor: Sizes 3,4 & 5 with outer material of Japanese Cordley Felt Yellow and  4 polyester  lin ings, latex balance bladder and butyl valve. Weight is 410-430g.

Futsal: Size 4 with outer material of Korean Duxon Shine, 3 polyester linings and  butyl bladder. White cotton filling for low bounce, with same design as the Premier Match outdoor balls and a weight of 380-400 g..

Volleyballs
Pro model. Outer material is Cordley Jet with 2 linings of polyester, circumference 64-65 cm and weight 280-300g.

Pro model: The outer cover of PU microfibre in yellow and black gives a softer touch.

Light weight: soft touch for use by recreational players and younger children. Made of PU 6500 with 1 lining of polyester and weight of 200-220g.

Pro Beach volleyball: Outer material is made with L-150 Foamy material and butyl bladder. It is the same size as regular volleyball weighing 280-300g. Yellow, blue and orange colours are used.

Rugby Balls

For dry weather, the outer material is L-150 Kid with 4 polyester linings and a latex balance bladder. For wet weather, there is a ball with a rubberized rough outer material providing better grip.
