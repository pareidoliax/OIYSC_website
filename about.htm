About Y FOCUS Fair Trade Sports
Goals and Objectives - Sales and Distribution - Participation 

Y FOCUS Fair Trade Sports is a project of the Y Service Club of Ottawa, part of a network of Y Service Clubs, individuals and community groups, and set up to import, distribute and sell Fairtrade sports balls as a vehicle to promote the principles of Fair Trade and to support financially, worthy peace building projects around the world. 

Note: The term Sports Ball includes balls for soccer, rugby, softball, baseball, volleyball, beach volleyball and fussball.

Goals and Objectives
Through the sale of various types of sports balls, manufactured by Fairtrade certified co-operatives and companies, we propose to do the following:

1. In our advertising and labeling, we would promote fair trading, as well as work for the elimination of child labour and the provision of decent wages, improved working conditions and adequate educational and health services for the workers in the industry.

Our awareness raising would also be achieved by working with other fair trade groups across Canada and through partnerships with other interested groups such as churches, service clubs and other community groups.

2. Through the Fairtrade premium built into the purchase price , we will provide the fair trade certified businesses from whom we buy, with funds to allow them to pay decent wages, to carry on without children under 15 and to provide medical and educational support to their workers. In addition, using the profit from the sales, we would raise funds for peace building through education, youth sports and skills training programs especially in war-torn countries where such activities will enhance the building of a culture of peace in the country. 

We will enlist the participation of supporting individuals and groups to carry out such activities as sending or purchasing education and sports supplies for schools in poorer countries or in poorer areas of developed countries such as Canada. 
 

Sales and Distribution
There will be a network of District Sales Agents each with a clearly defined territory and they will have the exclusive right to sell our Fair Trade certified sports products in those areas as a way to raise funds for outreach work in Canada or abroad. Groups approved as DSA's must be non-profit, community based and service oriented.

In addition, existing Fair Trade groups are able to add our products to their present merchandise list. Non-profit, community groups will also be able to sell balls as a fund raising venture to support their outreach activities. Local arrangements for this purpose would be with the local DSA, where there is one.

Participation in Y FOCUS Fair Trade Sports
Participation is open to individuals, groups, non-governmental organizations and businesses through the purchase of an interest in Y FOCUS Fair Trade and becoming a Fair Trade Partner.
The finacial interest will be considered a loan to Y FOCUS and may be redeemed at any time with written notice of 30 days. When we form a national co-operative to import and distribute the fair trade certified balls, such loans will be converted into shares.
