Here is some information about the country and the particular projects you will be helping with your donations.

The agency being supported is Salve Aide based in Ottawa .  Contact: salvaide@web.net

SalvAide’s mission is to build social and economic justice, democracy and dignity with the people of El Salvador. SalvAide is a registered Canadian charitable organization composed of a network of communities and supporters that stretches from coast to coast.

SALV AIDE'S GOALS ARE:

1.To increase the strength of civil society in El Salvador as represented by non- governmental

organizations (NGOs), popular organizations, women's organizations and communities.

2.To strengthen local economies in El Salvador which are participating in regional and national

efforts to create sustainable and diversified economic development.

3.To build Canadian support for community and democratic development in El Salvador.

SalvAide funds development projects in El Salvador designed by rural communities to meet their needs and priorities. Since 1985 SalvAide has funded such diverse projects as poultry farms, dairy herds, daycares, schools, community stores, medical clinics, cottage industries and legal aid clinics. SalvAide also funds human rights work and organizational development of Salvadorean non-governmental and popular organizations.

COUNTRY DESCRIPTION: El Salvador is a democratic country with a developing economy.  Tourism facilities are not fully developed.  The capital is San Salvador, accessible by El Salvador's International Airport at Comalapa.  Although the dollar and Colon are legal tender in El Salvador, in practice the Colon has fallen out of use.

Here is an example of one major new project:

La panadería IDEAL manejada por la juventud rural

"Ideal Bakery" an effort by rural youth

It has been an effort by the 3 micro-regions SES, MES and IDES of bajo lempa.

The bakery will give youth employment in the rural areas where 70% of households live in conditions of poverty or extreme poverty:

The operators work from 7 am. for eight hours. The sales persons start at 5 am and finishes at 8 am. The maintenance person helps with cleaning and prepares all the necessary items for the production. The person selling the pastry distributes the product in the 3 micro-regions. The supervisor helps by controlling the process and activities, as well as inventory, production, sales and purchasing.

The age average of the people from the bakery it is from 18 to 25 years old.
