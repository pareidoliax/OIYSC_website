             Let the Children Play
 

AGENCIES BEING SUPPORTED.

YSC Fair Trade Sports has provided soccer balls and other equipment to  a number of smaller Canadian based international agencies. They will also accept donations directly and provide receipts for tax purposes. They will also make sure all donations of sports equipment and educational supplies will be given to the children they work with, mainly through visits by their volunteers.

All general cash and material donations will be distributed among the agencies by YSC Fair Trade Sports unless there is a local group who wishes to support a locally based project. 

  • Accesso International with projects in the Caribbean, Central and South America.
  • Canadian Support for Rural African Initiatives working in Tanzania.
  • CanHave with a children’s centre in Uganda.
  • Childhaven with orphanages in Nepal and India.
  • AIDS Moncton and AIDS St John partnered with groups in Swaziland.
  • Freetown Y Service Club and Y FOCUS-Ottawa  in Sierra Leone. 
  • Wakefield Grannies with their project in South Africa.
  • Salv Aide with projects in El Salvador.
  • The Children's Bridge Foundation with orphanages in Vietnam, India and other Asian countries.
