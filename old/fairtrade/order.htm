Online (Small Quantities, 1-19 balls)
Small quantities of balls may be purchased from Fair World Sports Balls online if there is no distributor close by. These orders will normally be sent by Canada Post

Larger Orders
For larger orders can be ordered by the case (20 balls).   For all orders, e-mail us , listing the number of each quality of ball you require. Please include your name, shipping address and a phone number. We will contact you as soon as possible with a final price including taxes and shipping, and to arrange payment. Wholesale prices are available for orders of 40 or more balls for leagues and resale. Payment by credit card is now available through the YM-YWCA in Ottawa.

The suggested retail prices are kept below what you would pay for a sports ball of similar quality and the proceeds are used to support projects in the local community or internationally that deal with issues of peace building and eliminating violence.
