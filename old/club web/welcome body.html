Y FOCUS - Ottawa
You are welcome
The club usually meets  monthly for dinner at the Metro Y, 180  Argyla Ave on the fourth Monday, at 6.00 p.m. There is a different theme each month and usually we have a guest speaker with something interesting to share that will help us in our work. 
The second  Monday is usually a Planning or  a Business Night, usually at 7.00 p.m., to  work on projects, conduct business, plan for the future or discuss issues of special interest to the members. In addition we will organize a day or  weekend for a work party or other special fund raising or service project.

Our Mission statement says that Y FOCUS is people working together, using their time and talents, 
helping others develop their own resources and empowering them to take control over their own lives.

Our Service Goal is to focus primarily on the needs and rights of children while continuing to support
the programs of our international movement, the National Capital Region YMCA/YWCA and 
community groups who share similar goals and objectives.

This web site gives you a lot of information about our club. If what you read is what you are looking
for, please join us at one of our meetings, to share our fellowship, to offer service to your community
and to enjoy personal growth and development.
