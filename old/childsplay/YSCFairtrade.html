                                     YSC  Fair World Sports
Your source of  Fair Trade Sports balls in Canada, a national project of the Y Service Clubs of Canada.

Let the Children Play
 

YSC Fair World Sports is a national project of the Y Service Clubs of Canada which is establishing a network of Y Service Clubs, individuals and community groups, 
    - to sell Fair Trade sports balls across Canada, 
    - to promote the principles of Fair Trade and the elimination of child labour as well as 
    - to support financially, worthy peace building projects around the world. 

Y FOCUS Fair World Sports is the Ottawa based program of Y FOCUS-Ottawa (the Ottawa Y Service Club), with the right to sell Fair Trade certified sports products in the Ottawa Valley/ West Quebec area  and operates the overall project on behalf of all the Y Service Clubs in Canada.

Please visit the web site at www.ncf.ca/yfocus/fairtrade  to get more information on the program and Fair Trade principles and to see the products.
