Honduras.

Here is some information about the country and the particular projects you will be helping with your donations.

The agency being supported is ACCESO INTERNATIONAL based in Ottawa.

ACCESO International provides educational materials and bursary assistance for an orphanage in La Flecha, an orphanage in El Progreso, a primary school in the remote mountain village of Buen Ejemplo and a home work centre in Los Laureles. Through these four projects, over 400 primary level children are being individually assisted with shoes, packsacks, uniforms and nutrition, while their classrooms have become more colourful, creative and stimulating with many much needed school supplies. ACCESO International also helps a high school in El Progreso and a high school in La Lima whereby 3 young women are receiving full bursaries to attend these institutions and the classrooms and libraries of over 1500 high school students are being filled with supplies and books.
