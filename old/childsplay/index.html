     Let the Children Play

A Canada-wide project to provide soccer balls and other sports and school equipment for use in poor and war affected countries. We will collect used balls and other equipment as well as cash to purchase new Fair Trade certified soccer balls and education supplies, for schools, orphanages, refugee camps and community youth programs.

A project of the YSC Fair World Sports.
You can help children enjoy just being children, having fun, playing soccer or going to school, despite poverty, AIDS, hunger and family displacement.   Photos below.

 Your group can organize a series of special events such as Fair Trade soccer tournaments, soccer demonstrations and the collection of used soccer balls and other sports and educational equipment as well as cash donations.

Support  groups working with children and young people who may have been orphaned by AIDS or who have contracted AIDS, who are frustrated by the lack of education and jobs and who are losing hope that their life will ever be better. 

All funds raised will go to schools, YMCAs, orphanages and refugee camps, wherever children need some joy and hope in their lives. You can also help send new Fair Trade soccer balls and help end the exploitation of children as workers, so that they too can play.

The soccer balls and other equipment collected will be given to your chosen international project, involving children in poor and war affected countries.

A new or used soccer ball is an amazing gift to children who may never have seen a real soccer ball  but play with leaves or plastic bags tied up with string.
